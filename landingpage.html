History of QuantumGuard
Blockchain

Enhance security, safeguard your data, and embrace the future of quantum technology with QuantumGuard.
Threat from Quantum Computers
Quantum computers are becoming a reality, posing a serious threat to traditional cryptographic systems like RSA and ECC, compromising the security of transactions and data in current blockchains.
Solution
Recognizing this challenge, QuantumGuard Blockchain was developed to leverage post-quantum cryptography and advanced security methods.
Research Initiation
Research on QuantumGuard began in 2023, aiming to create a blockchain system capable of resisting attacks from quantum computers and adapting to future technologies.
Prototype Deployment
In mid-2024, the first prototype of QuantumGuard was deployed, marking a breakthrough in applying security solutions to blockchain.
Substrate Platform
Substrate, a flexible open-source platform, was chosen as the infrastructure for QuantumGuard due to its customization capabilities and ease of integration with quantum technologies.
Comprehensive Security Solution
QuantumGuard has evolved into a comprehensive quantum security solution, integrating QKD, PQC, and QRNG to protect against quantum attacks and adapt to new technologies.
